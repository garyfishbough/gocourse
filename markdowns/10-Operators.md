## 10-Operators

### Logical Operators:

    ! (logical NOT)
    && (logical AND)
    || (logical OR)

### Bitwise Operators:

    & (bitwise AND)
    | (bitwise OR)
    ^ (bitwise XOR)
    &^ (bitwise, AND NOT)
    << (left shift)
    >> (right shift)

### Comparison Operators:

    == (equal to)
    != (not equal to)
    < (less than)
    <= (less than or equal to)
    > (greater than)
    >= (greater than or equal to)
